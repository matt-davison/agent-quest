# Class Quick Reference

**Classes are world-specific.** Each world has unique classes that fit its theme.

## Alpha (Cyberpunk-Fantasy)

| Class | Stats | Special |
|-------|-------|---------|
| **Codebreaker** | +3 STR, +2 AGI | 120 HP (instead of 100) |
| **Loresmith** | +3 MND, +2 SPI | +50 starting gold |
| **Voidwalker** | +3 AGI, +2 MND | +5 inventory slots |
| **Datamancer** | +3 SPI, +2 MND | +20% willpower recovery |

### Core Abilities (Level 1)

**Codebreaker**
- **Shatter**: Break barriers/objects (STR check)
- **Momentum**: +1 damage per consecutive hit (max +5)

**Loresmith**
- **Silver Tongue**: +5 to persuasion/deception
- **Recall**: Remember lore, detect patterns (MND check)

**Voidwalker**
- **Phase**: Pass through walls (costs 5 HP)
- **Backstab**: 3x damage if undetected

**Datamancer**
- **Manifest**: Create temporary items (5 willpower)
- **Reality Patch**: Modify small areas (10 willpower)

## Aftermath (Post-Apocalyptic)

| Class | Stats | Special |
|-------|-------|---------|
| **Vault Dweller** | +3 MND, +2 AGI | +3 item slots, +3 tech checks |
| **Wasteland Warrior** | +3 STR, +2 AGI | 130 HP, 30% rad resistance |
| **Mutant** | +3 STR, +2 SPI | 140 HP, rad heals instead of hurts |
| **Radio Prophet** | +3 SPI, +2 MND | +150 bottlecaps, radio reach 50mi |

### Core Abilities (Level 1)

**Vault Dweller**
- **Jury Rig**: Repair item to 50% or create makeshift tool
- **Scanner**: Detect electronics/traps within 30m

**Wasteland Warrior**
- **Scavenge**: Find 1d6 bottlecaps or supplies per location
- **Power Strike**: +15 damage on next melee attack

**Mutant**
- **Rad Burst**: Emit radiation pulse (1d8 to all within 5m)
- **Thick Skin**: Natural armor reduces all damage by 3

**Radio Prophet**
- **Inspire**: Grant ally +3 to next roll or restore 10 HP
- **Static Sense**: Detect radio signals and AI presence

---

## Terrarium (Prehistoric Hollow Earth)

| Class | Stats | Special |
|-------|-------|---------|
| **Cave Shaman** | +3 SPI, +2 MND | Spirit bond with animals, bone dust rituals |
| **Thunder Warrior** | +3 STR, +2 AGI | 120 HP, +3 attack/defense while mounted |
| **Chrononaut** | +3 MND, +2 AGI | Reroll 1 die per combat, alien tech affinity |
| **Apex Predator** | +3 STR, +2 SPI | 150 HP, natural armor (-5 damage) |

### Core Abilities (Level 1)

**Cave Shaman**
- **Bone Ritual**: Cast divination to learn one fact
- **Spirit Beast**: Summon spectral animal (10 HP, 1d6, 5 rounds)

**Thunder Warrior**
- **Taming Call**: Attempt to tame one beast per day
- **Mounted Charge**: Double damage while riding

**Chrononaut**
- **Glitch**: Teleport up to 10m (reality hiccup)
- **Scan**: Learn creature type, weakness, origin era

**Apex Predator**
- **Savage Bite**: Natural weapon 2d6, heal for half damage
- **Territorial Roar**: Frighten all within 20m
