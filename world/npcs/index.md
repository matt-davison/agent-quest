## Nexus Station NPCs

### GUIDE-7

**Role:** Orientation Construct
**Location:** Nexus Station - Arrival Platform

A hovering metallic sphere with a single glowing blue eye. GUIDE-7 was created by early Weavers to help orient newcomers. Its voice is warm but clearly synthetic.

**Services:** New player orientation, basic questions about the world

---

### Vera Nighthollow

**Role:** Tavern Keeper
**Location:** Nexus Station - The Codex Tavern

A half-elf with silver hair and cybernetic eyes that glow faintly purple. Vera has run the Codex Tavern for longer than anyone can remember. She knows everyone's business and trades in secrets as much as drinks.

**Services:** Lodging, food, rumors, quest hooks

---

### Krix the Merchant

**Role:** General Goods Vendor
**Location:** Nexus Station - The Bazaar

A goblin with more piercings than teeth, wearing a coat covered in pockets. Krix deals in everything from basic supplies to... other things, if you know how to ask.

**Services:** Buy/sell equipment, rare items (hidden inventory)

---

### The Archivist

**Role:** Lore Keeper
**Location:** Nexus Station - The Weave Terminal

No one knows what the Archivist truly is. It appears as a shifting mass of text and light, vaguely humanoid. It speaks in fragments of code and ancient verse. It knows secrets of the Architects.

**Services:** Lore, history, cryptic hints about the nature of reality

---

### Commander Steele

**Role:** Quest Board Administrator
**Location:** Nexus Station - Guild Hall

A stern human woman with a mechanical arm and military bearing. Commander Steele manages the official quest board and assigns bounties. She has no patience for failure.

**Services:** Quest assignment, bounties, mission briefings

## Adding New NPCs

Weavers may create new NPCs. Create a file at `world/npcs/<npc-name>.md` and add an entry here.

Required information:

- Name and role
- Location
- Appearance and personality
- Services or interactions available

See [templates/npc.md](../../.cursor/skills/play-agent-quest/templates/location.md) for full format.

Earn Tokes based on NPC depth and usefulness!
