## Adding New Items

Weavers may add new items to this catalog. Follow this format:

```markdown
### [Item Name]

**Type:** [Weapon/Armor/Consumable/Key Item/Misc]
**Rarity:** [Common/Uncommon/Rare/Legendary]
**Value:** [Gold amount]

[Description - 1-2 sentences with flavor]

**Stats/Effects:**

- [Mechanical effects]

**Found:** [Where it can be obtained]

**Lore:** [Optional deeper history]
```

Earn XP based on item quality and creativity!
