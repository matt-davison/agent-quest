# Character Classes - Terrarium World

*Prehistoric Hollow Earth - 65 Million Years in a Biodome*

Each class in Terrarium reflects life in the absurd prehistoric hollow earth experiment. Dinosaurs, kaiju, cavemen, elves, and alien bureaucrats coexist in this vast underground biodome.

**All calculations must use the [math skill](../../.claude/skills/math/)**—especially for damage and HP.

## Cave Shaman

> *"The spirits of tooth and claw speak through the bones."*

**Role:** Primal magic user, spirit channeler, tribal healer

### Stats
- **+3 Spirit, +2 Mind**
- **Spirit Bond:** Can communicate with animals and sense their emotions
- Starting Reagents: **Bone Dust x5** (required for rituals)

### Passive: Ancestor's Whisper
Once per location, receive a cryptic warning from ancient spirits about danger. Additionally, you can read omens in bones, clouds, and animal behavior to predict near-future events.

### Class Abilities

| Tier | Ability | Cost | Effect |
|------|---------|------|--------|
| **1** | **Bone Ritual** | 1 Bone Dust | Cast divination to learn one fact about area or creature |
| **1** | **Spirit Beast** | 4 Spirit | Summon spectral animal companion (10 HP, 1d6 damage, 5 rounds) |
| **3** | **Primal Healing** | 3 Spirit | Touch heals 3d8 HP or cures poison/disease |
| **3** | **Earth Speak** | Free (1/day) | Commune with the land to find water, shelter, or hidden paths |
| **5** | **Totemic Ward** | 6 Spirit | Create protective circle (10m): allies inside gain +3 defense |
| **7** | **Ancestor Fury** | 8 Spirit | Channel ancient warrior: +5 to all stats for 3 rounds |
| **10** | **Kaiju Caller** | 15 Spirit | Summon a juvenile kaiju to fight for you (1 minute) |
| **15** | **World Tree Connection** | 20 willpower | Teleport party to any location you've visited |

### Magic Style
- Use bones, feathers, and blood as spell components
- Commune with spirits for knowledge and power
- Excel at healing and defensive magic
- Can calm or enrage wild beasts

### Starting Equipment
- **Bone Staff** (Melee Weapon, 1d8 damage) - Topped with prehistoric skull, channels spirit energy
- **Fur & Hide Armor** (Light Armor, -3 damage) - Blessed with protective runes
- **Spirit Fetishes** (Accessory) - +2 Spirit checks, counts as spell focus
- **Healing Salve x3** - Consumable: Heal 20 HP over 3 rounds
- **Bone Dust x5** - Required for rituals

### Weaknesses
- Physically fragile without animal allies
- Dependent on natural materials (can't cast in "dead" zones)
- Spirits sometimes demand payment (sacrifices, promises)

## Thunder Warrior

> *"My mount is 8 tons of muscle and rage. Your argument is invalid."*

**Role:** Dinosaur cavalry, mounted combatant, apex hunter

### Stats
- **+3 Strength, +2 Agility**
- Base HP: **120** (instead of 100)
- **Mounted Bonus:** +3 to attack and defense while riding a beast

### Passive: Beast Master
You can tame and ride any creature up to Large size (including dinosaurs). Tamed beasts are fiercely loyal and gain +10 HP. You can communicate basic commands to any beast, even if not tamed.

### Class Abilities

| Tier | Ability | Cost | Effect |
|------|---------|------|--------|
| **1** | **Taming Call** | Free (1/day) | Attempt to tame one beast (opposed Spirit check) |
| **1** | **Mounted Charge** | 3 Spirit | While riding: charge attack deals double damage |
| **3** | **Pack Tactics** | Passive | You and your mount both attack on your turn |
| **3** | **Thunder Stomp** | 5 HP | Your mount stomps: 2d8 damage to all adjacent enemies |
| **5** | **Primal Bond** | Free (1/mount) | Permanent bond with one beast: share HP pool, telepathic link |
| **7** | **Apex Roar** | 6 Spirit | Your mount roars: frighten all enemies (Spirit check) |
| **10** | **Stampede** | 10 Spirit | Summon 1d6 wild dinosaurs to rampage through battlefield |
| **15** | **Kaiju Imprint** | 15 Spirit | Bond with a juvenile kaiju as your mount (permanent until death) |

### Combat Style
- Devastating mounted charges
- High mobility on swift dinosaurs
- Mount acts as both weapon and shield
- Can switch between mounts for different situations

### Starting Equipment
- **Stone Spear** (Melee/Thrown Weapon, 1d10 damage) - Obsidian tip, can be thrown 30m
- **Dinosaur Hide Armor** (Medium Armor, -4 damage) - Scales and bone plates
- **War Horn** (Accessory) - Call your mount from up to 1 mile away
- **Taming Net** - +2 to beast taming checks
- **Raptor Mount** (Starting Beast) - Fast scout mount (40 HP, 1d8 claws, 15m movement)

### Weaknesses
- Heavily reliant on mount (vulnerable when dismounted)
- Large mounts can't enter tight spaces
- Mounts require food (a LOT of food for large dinosaurs)

## Chrononaut

> *"I'm not from around here. Or around 'now,' for that matter."*

**Role:** Time-displaced traveler, alien tech user, reality glitcher

### Stats
- **+3 Mind, +2 Agility**
- **Temporal Instability:** Once per combat, reroll any one die
- Starting Equipment: **Alien Gadget (random)** - mysterious device with unknown purpose

### Passive: Unstuck in Time
You occasionally receive flashes of past or future events. At any moment, you may ask the GM "Have I seen this before?" for a hint. Additionally, aging effects and time-based magic don't affect you.

### Class Abilities

| Tier | Ability | Cost | Effect |
|------|---------|------|--------|
| **1** | **Glitch** | 3 Spirit | Teleport up to 10m instantly (reality hiccup) |
| **1** | **Scan** | Free (1/target) | Analyze creature/object: learn type, weakness, origin era |
| **3** | **Time Skip** | 5 willpower | Rewind last round of combat (undo everyone's actions) |
| **3** | **Phase Shift** | 4 Spirit | Become intangible for 1 round (cannot attack or be attacked) |
| **5** | **Future Sight** | 6 Spirit | See 3 rounds ahead: auto-dodge one attack, +3 to one roll |
| **7** | **Probability Manipulation** | 8 willpower | Turn one failed roll into a critical success |
| **10** | **Stop Time** | 12 Spirit | Freeze time for 1 round (only you can act) |
| **15** | **Paradox Engine** | 15 willpower | Summon your past/future self as ally (3 rounds, full stats) |

### Exploration Style
- Navigate using alien technology
- Sense temporal anomalies and dimensional weak points
- Confused by primitive culture (advantage: knowledge of future tech)
- Can identify and use alien artifacts instinctively

### Starting Equipment
- **Chrono-Blaster** (Ranged Weapon, 1d10 energy damage) - Alien sidearm, unlimited ammo
- **Enviro-Suit** (Light Armor, -2 damage) - Self-cleaning, climate control, HUD displays
- **Alien Translator** (Accessory) - Understand all languages (even dinosaur body language)
- **Ration Cube x10** - Compressed future food (each restores 10 HP)
- **Random Alien Gadget** - Roll 1d6 for effect when used (unknown until triggered)

### Weaknesses
- Alien tech can malfunction in this primitive environment
- Temporal instability causes occasional glitches (GM-controlled)
- Culturally out of place (primitive peoples fear or worship you)

## Apex Predator

> *"I am the food chain."*

**Role:** Kaiju-bonded berserker, monstrous shapeshifter, living disaster

### Stats
- **+3 Strength, +2 Spirit**
- Base HP: **150** (highest starting HP in Terrarium)
- **Monstrous Resilience:** Natural armor: reduce all damage by 5

### Passive: Hunter's Instinct
Automatically track any creature you've previously encountered. Sense fear in others (+3 to intimidation). Once per day, enter **Feral State**: gain +5 Strength but lose ability to speak for 1 hour.

### Class Abilities

| Tier | Ability | Cost | Effect |
|------|---------|------|--------|
| **1** | **Savage Bite** | Free (1/combat) | Natural weapon: 2d6 damage, heal for half damage dealt |
| **1** | **Territorial Roar** | 4 Spirit | Frighten all enemies within 20m (Spirit check) |
| **3** | **Predator's Strike** | 6 Spirit | Attack deals triple damage to frightened targets |
| **3** | **Pack Alpha** | Passive | Beasts you encounter are either submissive or challenge you (never neutral) |
| **5** | **Partial Transformation** | 8 HP | Grow claws/fangs: +10 damage, +5 HP, 4 rounds |
| **7** | **Regeneration** | 10 Spirit | Heal 5 HP per round for 5 rounds |
| **10** | **Full Transformation** | 15 willpower | Become a kaiju: double HP, +15 damage, 10 rounds |
| **15** | **Apex Form** | 20 willpower | Permanent kaiju transformation (can revert at will) |
| **20** | **King of Monsters** | 30 willpower | Ultimate form: 500 HP, devastating attacks, 1 minute |

### Combat Style
- Overwhelming physical power
- Can tank enormous amounts of damage
- Transformation abilities scale with level
- Natural weapons (claws, fangs, horns)

### Starting Equipment
- **Bone Claws** (Natural Weapon, 1d12 damage) - Protruding from your knuckles
- **Thick Hide** (Natural Armor, -5 damage) - Scaly or chitinous skin
- **Predator's Trophy** (Accessory) - Wear skull of defeated alpha: +2 to intimidation
- **Raw Meat x5** - Consumable: Restore 15 HP (you prefer it bloody)

### Weaknesses
- Low social skills (intimidation works, diplomacy doesn't)
- Transformation costs can be steep
- Risk of losing control in Feral State
- Feared and hunted by settlements

## Multi-Class Synergies

When surviving in the Terrarium:

- **Cave Shaman + Thunder Warrior:** Spirit magic enhances beast bonds
- **Thunder Warrior + Apex Predator:** Mounted and monstrous—unstoppable duo
- **Chrononaut + Cave Shaman:** Future tech meets ancient magic
- **Apex Predator + Chrononaut:** Temporal kaiju from different eras

## Progression

Terrarium has a higher level cap (20) than other worlds. As you survive and evolve:

### Milestones

| XP Milestone | Unlock |
|--------------|--------|
| 500 | Enhanced basic abilities |
| 1000 | Passive ability empowered |
| 2500 | Unlock Tier 15 abilities |
| 5000 | Evolution: Class-specific transformation |
| 10000 | Unlock Tier 20 abilities (max level) |

### Enhanced Passives (at 1000 XP)

- **Cave Shaman:** Ancestor's Whisper provides specific warnings (not just cryptic ones)
- **Thunder Warrior:** Beast Master: Can bond with two mounts simultaneously
- **Chrononaut:** Temporal Instability: Reroll up to 3 dice per combat (was 1)
- **Apex Predator:** Hunter's Instinct: Track any creature across any terrain, even through portals

## World Note

Terrarium has unique features:
- **Level Cap 20** (higher than Alpha/Aftermath's 10)
- **Alien Oversight:** Bureaucrats sometimes intervene in absurd ways
- **Kaiju Events:** Massive creatures periodically rampage through zones
- **Time Distortions:** Different areas may experience time at different rates
